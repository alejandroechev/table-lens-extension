📄 Real-World Document Testing

These document simulations represent common OCR challenges encountered in business environments:

Document Types Included:

  • Scanned Memos: Standard office documents with rotation and scanning artifacts
  • Poor Quality Scans: Documents with dust, smudges, and uneven lighting
  • Fax Transmissions: Low resolution with transmission artifacts and scan lines
  • Typewritten Reports: Vintage documents with uneven character alignment
  • Thermal Receipts: Small fonts with thermal printing characteristics
  • Medical Reports: Formal medical documentation with precise data tables
  • Technical Specifications: Dense engineering data with small fonts and symbols

Common Real-World Challenges:

  • Document rotation and skewing
  • Inconsistent lighting and shadows
  • Paper texture and aging effects
  • Mixed font sizes and families
  • Complex table structures
  • Faded or worn text
  • Scanning artifacts and compression

Testing Tips: Try selecting different table areas from various document types to test how well your OCR handles different real-world scenarios.
