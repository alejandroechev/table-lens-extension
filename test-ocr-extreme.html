⚠️ Extreme Test Guidelines

Warning: These tests are designed to challenge OCR systems beyond normal operating conditions.

Test Scenarios Include:

  • Ultra Low Contrast: Text barely visible against background
  • Pattern Interference: Complex background patterns that confuse recognition
  • Font Extremes: Ultra-thin, condensed, and pixelated fonts
  • Lighting Effects: Uneven lighting, vignettes, and spotlights
  • Visual Distortions: Waves, blurs, rotations, and skewing
  • Overlay Interference: Watermarks and semi-transparent overlays
  • Color Vision Issues: Simulated color blindness scenarios
  • Screen Artifacts: CRT effects, scan lines, and digital noise
  • Print Simulation: Faded photocopies and aging effects
  • Combined Challenges: Multiple difficulties simultaneously

Expected Results:

Most OCR systems will struggle with these tests. Success rates may be significantly lower than standard tests. Use these to:

  • Identify the limits of your OCR system
  • Test error handling and graceful degradation
  • Evaluate backup content analysis methods
  • Benchmark improvements in recognition algorithms

Note: These extreme conditions are rarely encountered in normal usage but help ensure robust performance in edge cases.
