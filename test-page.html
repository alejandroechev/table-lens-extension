üîß Troubleshooting:

  • Tables not detected: Try refreshing the page and clicking "Scan for Tables"
  • Chart not generating: Make sure you've selected both X-axis and Y-axis columns
  • Export not working: Ensure your browser allows downloads from extensions
  • Performance issues: Large tables (>100 rows) may take longer to process
